Solutions to homelessness

  1. Federal Housing Assistance:

    2. The Federal Housing Assistance is one of the many ways to reduce homelessness because of the varity and uniqe options it provides. Housing Choice Vouchers and section 8 vouchers; these are vouchers that provide low-income families modest market-rating housing. This provides a a flexable subsidy that adjust with the family's income over time. This ensures that many families reamin stable in a home other than the shelter. One major concern is the wailist for these vouchers are intense. Many housing agencies have closed their waiting list becasue of how many applicants applied. Housing assistance provides many families who pay have their earnign for rent because of how expensive it is to live in New York.

  2. Permanent Supportive Housing:

    3. This model combines affordable housing with suppot service and individuals living with mental illness and health conditions. Reasearch suggest that permanent supportive housing costs less than other forms of emergency and institutional care. New York Agreement is well known supportive housing initiative that successfully reduced homelessness in New York City and saved taxpayer dollars that would otherwise have been spent on costly shelters and hospitalizations. PSH mostly targets individuals who live in unstable housing and push towards a better future. Most resediance are suported by non profit organization.

  3. "Housing First":

    4. The Housing First approach includes moving homeless individuals directly into subsidized housing and then linking them to support services, either on-site or in the community. This gives a stable housing system in the long term for homeless individuals. This approach is far less costly than emergency and institutional care,such as shelters, hospitals and correctional facilities. Statistics show that 80% of residents have successfully remained in housing for 3 years. To add on, 95% have a stable income. While there are many homeless New Yorkers still in the streets putting them on the on the right path goes a long way.
